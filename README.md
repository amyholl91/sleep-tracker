## Data Storage

All sleep data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage. Your data never leaves your device.

**Important:** 
- Back up your data regularly using the "Download Backup" button in Settings
- Your data won't transfer if you switch browsers or devices
- Clearing browser data will delete your sleep logs
